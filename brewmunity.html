What is Bremunity?

    Brew-mu-ni-ty (N.)
  • 1. A group of people having a favorite craft brew, flavor, hop, malt, or yeast characteristic in common.
  • 2. A feeling of fellowship with others as a result of sharing hand crafted brews, interests, and goals.
